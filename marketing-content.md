## How It Works: Your 3-Step Journey to a Deployed App

1. **Upload Your Vision:** Start by uploading a simple text file, markdown document, or even a PDF describing what you want to build. No complex specifications needed. Just write in plain English.

2. **Intelligent Analysis & Clarification:** Our AI agent reads your requirements, analyses the key features, and asks clarifying questions to ensure it understands your vision completely. It then builds a comprehensive knowledge graph of your application.

3. **Generate, Deploy, and Iterate:** With your approval, Command Agent v3 generates the complete full-stack application—frontend, backend, database, and all. You can download the code, push it to GitHub, or deploy a live marketing website and application preview in a single click.

## Features: More Than Just a Code Generator

- **Full-Stack Application Generation:** Get a complete codebase with a modern tech stack (React, Node.js, PostgreSQL) and best practices built-in.
- **AI-Powered Requirements Analysis:** Our agent understands your needs and identifies key features, technical requirements, and potential issues before a single line of code is written.
- **Interactive Knowledge Graph:** Visualise your application's architecture and data flow before it's built.
- **Automated Marketing Website:** Get a professional, auto-generated marketing website to showcase your new application.
- **Instant Deployment:** Deploy your application and marketing site to a live URL for testing and feedback in seconds.
- **Iterative Development via Chat:** Need to make changes? Just chat with the agent to modify your application after it has been generated.
